# Code of Conduct

We want platformkit-community to be useful, respectful, and safe for both new
users and experienced contributors.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and specific.
- Challenge ideas without attacking people.
- Keep discussions relevant and actionable.
- Do not harass, discriminate, or publish private information.
- Do not post exploit details or sensitive security information publicly.

## Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, remove, or lock content that violates these standards and
may restrict participation when needed to keep the community safe and useful.

## Reporting

If you need to report unacceptable behavior, use the support or security paths
documented in this repository. For sensitive matters, follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
